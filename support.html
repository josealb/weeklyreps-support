Frequently Asked Questions

How do I set my rep targets?

Run a max test for each exercise. The app will automatically calculate your per-set targets based on your results using proven training multipliers.

When does my weekly cycle reset?

Your weekly cycle runs Monday to Sunday. Once a workout is completed for the week, it locks until the following Monday.

What's included in the free version?

Push-ups tracking is free forever, including the max test and progress history for push-ups.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to Settings on your iPhone, tap your name, then Subscriptions. Find WeeklyReps and tap Cancel Subscription. Your access continues until the end of the current billing period.

I subscribed but my workouts are still locked. What do I do?

Open WeeklyReps, go to the paywall screen, and tap "Restore Purchase". If the issue persists, please contact us at the email above.

Is my data stored online?

No. All your workout data is stored locally on your device. We don't collect or transmit any of your workout information.
